Barbecue Ribs

Swet and spicyKansas City-Style Barbecue Ribs

Bucketloads of flavour and juicy fall-off-the-bone meat is all you can really hope for when it comes to barbecue ribs! Thankfully, this oven baked barbecue ribs tick all of the boxes.

Ingredients:

  • 4 pounds pork spareribs
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • ½ cup chile sauce
  • ¼ cup ketchup
  • ¼ cup soy sauce
  • ¼ cup Worcestershire sauce
  • ¼ cup rum
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 teaspoon dry mustard
  • 1 dash ground black
  • cooking spray

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Cut spareribs into serving size portions; wrap in double thickness of foil
  3. Bake in the preheated oven for 1 ½ hours. Unwrap and drain drippings. Place ribs in a large roasting pan
  4. Mix brown sugar, chile sauce, ketchup, soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, rum, garlic, mustard, and pepper together in a bowl. Coat ribs with sauce and marinate at room temperature for 1 hour or refrigerate for 8 hours to overnight
  5. Mix brown sugar, chile sauce, ketchup, soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, rum, garlic, mustard, and pepper together in a bowl. Coat ribs with sauce and marinate at room temperature for 1 hour or refrigerate for 8 hours to overnight
